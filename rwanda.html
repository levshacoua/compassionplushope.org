Focus Area
Gatare Village & Surrounding

In the heart of the "Land of a Thousand Hills," local communities thrive on resilience and faith. Despite significant progress, poverty rates in rural areas remain high. Many families still struggle with consistent access to clean water, secondary education, and specialized medical care. Our mission focuses on sustainable, long-term support that empowers these villages to flourish.

The Challenge

Why Help is Needed

Food Insecurity

Rural regions often experience erratic rainfall, affecting crop yields and basic nutrition.

Poverty

Many families live on less than $2 a day, making it difficult to afford school or care.

Education Gaps

While primary school is accessible, many drop out before completing secondary education.

Healthcare Access

Specialized medical treatments require traveling to major cities, which is unaffordable for many.

Community Rebuilding

Empowering village leaders to create sustainable local economies through micro-loans.

Leadership

Meet the Mission

Our work in Rwanda was born from a faith-driven calling to Gatare Village. We don't just send aid; we build long-term relationships with village elders and families. By listening first, we ensure that every dollar donated addresses the actual needs identified by the community itself.
