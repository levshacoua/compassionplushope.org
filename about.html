Origin

Our Story

Compassion Plus Hope began as a simple response to a profound calling. What started as a single mission trip to a remote village in Rwanda has grown into a cross-continental commitment to some of the world's most vulnerable populations.

Our growth into Ukraine followed the same principle: listening to local leaders and responding with practical, faith-rooted action. We don't just see nations; we see neighbors. Our journey is one of deepening relationships and expanding hope, one nation at a time.

FAQ

Common Questions

How are donations used?
Administrative costs are covered separately so that every dollar of your designated donation reaches those in need in the field.
Is my giving tax-deductible?
Yes, Compassion Plus Hope is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent of the law.
How do I know my money reaches those in need?
We provide regular financial reports and impact updates directly from our field directors in Rwanda and Ukraine.
How does monthly giving work?
Monthly supporters provide the stable funding we need to sustain long-term programs like our schools and feeding centers.
Are mission trips available?
We organize annual trips for partners and supporters to see the work firsthand. These are relationship-focused and high-impact.
Can I volunteer?
Yes! We offer volunteer opportunities both domestically and in the field. Contact our volunteer coordinator to learn more.
